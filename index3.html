Maya Ives

Age: 30 years old
Home country: India
Occupation: Fashion designer and writter

Maya is a young fashon designer that wants to protect the environment and planet earth while creating clothes for everyone.
Since a young age Maya has felt she has a strong tie to the environment, being born in a small village close to Mumbay.
When she moved to England to study, she was inspired to create an ecological brand, that creates clothes from 100% recycled textiles. For 5 years she has been living her dream of creating clothes and helping the planet.
This website was created to give insight into her brand and help people purchase Maya's clothes, but also give the ability to contact her directly.

This is the main logo that represents Maya Ives fashion design collection for man shirts. Logo was inspired by one of the most beloved animals in indian culture, the elephant. Symbolicly, the elephant primarily means strength, honor, stability and patience, among other attributes.
Maya Ives Fashion House logo was created having the idea of masculinity in mind, but also represent femininity, as the shirt created are ment for both genders.
Maya Ives is considered a global leader in the design, marketing and distribution of premium lifestyle products.
For almost 5 years, this brand's reputation and distinctive image have been consistently developed across an expanding number of products, brands and international markets.
At Maya Ives we pursue to be Eco-friendly in everything we do. We work hard to make our products duty, to protect the environment we live in.

We love trees and we have a vision of world where people are more connected to the nature. For every shirt sold we plant a tree.
Every plant will be planted in Africa in order to improve the natural landscape by preserving and expanding forest.
